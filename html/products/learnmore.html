Excellence in Global Oil Trading

Story of Esher Oils

At Esher Oils, our story is one of dedication, quality, and connection. From the moment we began our journey, our mission has been clear: to bridge the gap between the world's best oil producers and the businesses that rely on them. We specialize in the supply of premium Soybean, Olive, and Sunflower oils, serving a wide network of partners with a commitment to quality that sets us apart in the global oil trading industry.

Global Presence

Our headquaters are in Finland, but our reach extends far beyond. With presence in Ukraine, Portugal, Poland, and Germany, we are proud to be a trusted leader in the international oil market. Our presence across Europe allows us to build strong relationships with producers and customers alike, ensuring that every drop we deliver meets the highest standards of quality and sustainability. At Esher Oils, we don't just provide oils; we deliver peace of mind through rigorous quality control measures that safeguard the premium nature of our products.

Commitment to Sustainability

Sustainability lies at the heart of everything we do. Our dedication to environmentally responsible sourcing and trading means that our partners can rest assured that they are making a responsible choice. We believe in growing not only our business but also in contributing positively to the communities and ecosystems we engage with.

Expert Team

Our team consists of highly qualified industry experts with decades of combined experience in international supply chains. Their expertise guarantees seamless transactions and reliable partnerships that can withstand the ever-changing dynamics of the global market. We strive to lead with excellence, ensuring that our customers receive nothing less than the very best service, every single time.

Your Trusted Partner

Whether you're a manufacturer, distributor, or food producer, Esher Oils is here to support your needs. We are more than just a supplier—we are a partner committed to building lasting relationships through consistent quality, dependable service, and a passion for excellence in every drop we deliver.
